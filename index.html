Our Grading Rules

Please be honest when booking. It saves time for both sides and helps us pay quickly for good stock.

Grade A — Best Rate

Premium Resale Quality

  • Clean and fresh
  • Excellent condition
  • Branded or highly resaleable
  • No visible damage
  • Modern, fashionable and wearable
Grade B — Lower Rate

Still Wearable

  • Slightly used
  • Small visible signs of wear
  • Minor defects only
  • Still clean and wearable
  • May be accepted at a reduced rate
Grade C — Refused

Not Accepted

  • Ripped, broken or heavily worn
  • Stained, smelly, wet or mouldy
  • Unsellable, dirty or poor condition
  • Mixed household textiles
  • Will not be paid for
